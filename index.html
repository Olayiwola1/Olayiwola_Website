Land_Fix

Worked in a group of four during NSBE Hacks UofT in Toronto. Our idea was to help deal with the prominent problem of corruption around properties in Africa. We came up with a Blockchain based app that creates a digital record of the coordinates of lands/ properties and connects it to the rightful owners name. We also leveraged Google Maps API to create a visual representation of the owner's property. This system helps promote tansparency since it is immutable, it can only be changed when the ownership of the property is being passed on to another person.

Technologies Used:

HTML/CSS, JavaScript, Google Maps API, Python

Gamification

SHacks was Scotiabank's first ever hackathon. My team and I were tasked with creating a gamification system that can be used to help attract more customers. We approached the problem by trying to leverage AR technology and use it to help us market their services. We decided to use the bank's logo as an image marker, so whenever it is scanned by our app a trivia quiz would pop up and customers can play and learn more about the bank and their services.
